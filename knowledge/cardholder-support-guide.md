# CashPortal Cardholder Support Guide

Use this guide to answer customer questions about account access, activation, balances, card actions, profile settings, fees, transaction history, transfer history, and fund transfers.

## Portal Overview

CashPortal gives cardholders access to prepaid card account functions.

### Secondary Balance

The Secondary Balance holds funds securely until the cardholder transfers them to Card Balance from the Transfer Funds section. Funds in Secondary Balance cannot be used for ATM withdrawals or POS purchases until they are transferred to Card Balance. Transfers depend on the available card limits.

### Card Balance

Card Balance is the available balance on the card. Funds in Card Balance can be used for ATM withdrawals and POS purchases.

### Transaction History

Cardholders can view and monitor their latest transactions and card activity. The Transaction History page shows full transaction history since card activation, including purchase history and funds received.

### Transfer History

Transfer History shows transfer transaction history associated with the prepaid card account, including transferred funds and requested funds.

### Card Status

Cardholders can view whether their card status is normal, active, suspended, or inactive.

### CVV Code

If a cardholder does not remember their CVV code, they can use the CVV show link in the portal to view it.

### Request Physical Card

Cardholders with a virtual card can request a plastic physical card by selecting Request Physical Card. The card usually arrives in approximately 7 to 15 business days. The cardholder can confirm their postal address and choose regular post or courier. Courier can fast track delivery and is recommended for countries with poor local postal systems.

### Lost Card

If a card is lost or stolen, the cardholder can select Lost or Stolen Card. This automatically suspends the card and notifies Customer Service. A support agent will contact the cardholder with next steps, generally within 24 hours.

## Log In To Your Account

After receiving the activation email, the cardholder should:

1. Go to the secure portal login page.
2. Enter their email address or SAN.
3. Enter the access code from the activation email.
4. Complete the captcha checkbox.
5. Select Login.

### Forgot Access Code

If the cardholder forgot their access code, they can retrieve it from the login page by selecting Forgot Access Code.

### Access Code Does Not Work

If the access code does not work, the cardholder should contact support to have a new code issued.

## Activation Page

After logging in successfully, the cardholder reaches the activation page. They must fill in all required bolded fields, then select Activate.

## Manage My Account

Manage My Account is the cardholder homepage. It directs the cardholder to the available account functions.

Typical areas include:

- My Account
- My Profile
- Transaction history
- Limits
- Fees
- Transfer History
- Transfer Funds
- Lost or Stolen Card
- Request Physical Card

## Convert To Plastic

To convert a virtual card to a plastic card, the cardholder should go to the homepage, select Request Physical Card, and follow the steps. The plastic card usually arrives 10 to 15 business days from the time it is requested.

## Report Lost Or Stolen Card

If the cardholder lost their card or the card was stolen, they should select Lost or Stolen Card. Customer Service will be notified and will contact the cardholder directly with next steps.

## Retrieve My PIN

If the cardholder does not remember their four digit PIN, they can select Retrieve PIN on the portal homepage. A fee of 2.00 GBP, USD, or EUR applies for each PIN retrieval. Retrieve PIN should only be visible after the user has a physical card. The PIN is provided free of charge when the cardholder activates the physical card.

## Activate My Plastic Card

After receiving a plastic card in the mail, the cardholder must activate it in the portal.

Steps:

1. Go to My Account.
2. Select Activate Plastic Card.
3. Enter the new three digit CVV code from the back of the plastic card.

After activation, the CVV code on the virtual card automatically changes to the CVV code on the plastic card, allowing the cardholder to continue using the virtual card online.

## Upgrade

The Upgrade page allows the cardholder to upgrade their card by validating their account.

### Upgrade Your Card

The Upgrade Your Card section guides cardholders through the steps required to upgrade to stage 2. The user is guided to upload proof of address and ID.

## Fees

The Fees page shows possible fees associated with the cardholder account. The List of Fees shows standard banking fees charged to the cardholder.

## My Profile

The My Profile page allows cardholders to edit personal details, such as home address and phone number. Cardholders are not permitted to change their country because of AML rules and regulations.

## Transfer Funds

To transfer funds, the cardholder should select Secondary Balance -> Card. This lets users transfer funds from Secondary Balance to Card Balance. Users must follow the available limits when entering the transfer amount on the next page.

## Support Boundaries

Do not ask for full card numbers, passwords, one-time passcodes, or government identification numbers. If an action requires account-specific verification, direct the user to CashPortal support or the appropriate secure CashPortal flow.
